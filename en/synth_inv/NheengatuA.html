Synthesis Notes

This synthesis relies on Lima Schwade's description, which is the only one available for this variety of Nheengatú. Following this author's description, there are no [ND]s, either phonemic or allophonic, in médio Rio Amazônas Nheengatú. We assume underlying oral and nasal vowels and underlying /N, D/. We assume that /a/ is nasalized word-finally after /n/. We suspect that other vowels may also undergo nasalization in this environment, but await further empirical evidence to see whether this hypothesis is supported.
