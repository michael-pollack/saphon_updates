Synthesis Notes

This synthesis closely follows dos Santos' analysis of nasality in Karipuna. Our analysis crucially differs in that we further posit a process of LO, whereby /m, n, ŋ/ --> [mb, nd, ŋɡ] before an oral vowel. Though it is not possible to be certain that this process is really at play, the available transcriptions offered by the author suggest that this is indeed the case. There is one exception to this rule on p.5, table 5 /o-maʔẽ-maʔẽ/ --> [ma’ʔẽ-ma’ʔẽ] ‘wake up’, which remains unaccounted for, suggesting there may be variation with respect to the application of this particular process.
