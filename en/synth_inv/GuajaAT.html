Synthesis Notes

Our analysis is significantly informed by Cunha 1987, and we propose the following rules: (1) optional post oralization of /m,n/ when preceding oral V; (2) leftward LDNH triggered by stressed root final nasal Ṽ, with oral V and glides as undergoers and obstruents as blockers; (3) bidirectional nasalization of vowels adjacent to nasal consonants. Regarding (3), we note that Cunha divides this process into two: (i) nasalization of oral V when preceding nasal m,n; (ii) nasalization of oral V when following word initial m,n; however, we assume that these two procesesses are actually just part of a more general processes of LN:V.
