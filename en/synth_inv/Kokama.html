Synthesis Notes

We mostly follow Vallejos' description of Kokama, but supplement with several phonological processes discussed by Pike & Faust (vowel excrescence, vowel lengthening, and vowel devoicing). There are no salient nasal-oral allophonic processes, though there is some evidence to believe that vowels are slightly nasalized before nasal consonants, following some transcriptions of Viegas. We analyze /kʷ/ as a phoneme, rather than as a sequence of /ku/ as Vallejos does.
