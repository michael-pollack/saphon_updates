Notas

The segment given in the inventory as /ɺ/ is described by Hawkins (1998: 25) as an “/r/...formed with the blade of the tongue..an alveopalatal with popped lateral release”, and by Derbyshire (1999: 27) as “palatal ɹ with lateral release”.
